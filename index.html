Features

Everything your store needs

One plugin, zero configuration headaches. Works automatically the moment an order is placed.

💸

Native PIX QR Code

Generates a real PIX QR Code using the official Central Bank of Brazil format — scannable by any banking app.

🌍

SWIFT / IBAN Support

For international stores: bank name, SWIFT/BIC code, account number and IBAN — automatically included when currency is not BRL.

📧

Auto Email Attachment

The PDF invoice is automatically attached to the WooCommerce order confirmation email sent to your customer.

📥

Customer Download Area

A secure download button appears on the order page in My Account — customers can download their invoice anytime.

🎨

3 Beautiful Templates

Choose between Blue (classic), Modern (purple) and Minimalist (dark) — all professional and print-ready.

🏷️

White-label on PRO

On paid plans, the "Generated by SwiftInvoice" footer is automatically removed — fully branded invoices for your customers.

Setup

Up and running
in 5 minutes.

1

Install the plugin

Download from WordPress.org and install directly from your WooCommerce dashboard — no coding required.

2

Get your free API key

Create a free account on RapidAPI and subscribe to the BASIC plan — 50 free invoices per month, no credit card required.

3

Configure your details

Go to WooCommerce → SwiftInvoice, paste your API key, enter your company details, PIX key or bank info and choose a template.

4

Done — invoices generate automatically

Every new order triggers a PDF invoice, attached to the customer email and available for download in My Account.
